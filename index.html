Benyou Wang

@ Tencent
Benyou Wang is a Research Associate in Tencent Shenzhen, China. Before joining Tencent in July. 2017, he has been a Research Assisstant at Tianjin University since Feb. 2017. Benyou received his Master degree in Feb. 2017 from School of Computer Science and technology of Tianjin University. Before that, he received a B.Sc degree from the huat in 2014. Benyou's research interests include (but not limited) information retrieval, natural language processing , machine learning and quantum mechanics. He is now working on many textual problems, models, algorithms and innovations. Particularly,
  • Deep Learning for NLP (e.g. Question Answering, Image caption, Word Similarity, Sematic matching)
  • Information Retrieval (e.g. Query Expansion, Recomendation System, Session Search)
  • Quantum inspired Models in Language (e.g. Quantum language model)
My work on question answering and query wxpansion has been part of many Tencent products and services, including Tencent Cloud, Tencent HR customer service. And some work about Q-zone short vedio stream Recomendation will be deployed latter.
