Benyou Wang

@ Tencent
Benyou Wang is a Research Associate in Tencent Shenzhen, China. Before joining Tencent in July. 2017, he has been a Research Assisstant at Tianjin University since Feb. 2017. Benyou received his Master degree in Feb. 2017 from School of Computer Science and technology of Tianjin University, supervides by Professor Dawei Song and Doctor Peng Zhang . Before that, he received a B.Sc degree from the huat in 2014. Benyou's research interests include (but not limited) information retrieval, natural language processing , machine learning and quantum mechanics. He is now working on many textual problems, models, algorithms and innovations. Particularly,
  • Deep Learning for NLP (e.g. Question Answering, Image caption)
  • Information Retrieval (e.g. Query Expansion, Recomendation System, Session Search)
  • Quantum inspired Models in Language (e.g. Quantum language model)
  • Adversarial Learning and Reinforce Learning
My work on question answering and query expansion has been part of many Tencent products and services, including Tencent Cloud, Tencent HR customer service. And some work about Q-zone short vedio stream Recomendation will be deployed latter.

Recently, we try to use extra CCG syntax information for image caption. Before this we have tried joint task of image caption and machine translation in a cross-language, in which decoder is shared while differs in encoder-side (image or a cross language).
